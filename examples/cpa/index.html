How I Can Help You

From tax prep to business startup, I'll handle the paperwork so you don't have to

Personal Tax Returns

I'll handle all your personal tax paperwork—W-2s, 1099s, deductions, whatever you've got. Simple returns start at $125, and I'll make sure you get every credit you deserve.

Small Business Support

Starting a business? I'll help you choose the right structure (LLC, Corp, etc.), get your paperwork filed, and keep you compliant. From startup to tax season, I've got you covered.

Monthly Bookkeeping

Keep your business books clean and organized. I'll set up QuickBooks, handle monthly reconciliation, and make sure everything's ready for tax time. Sleep better knowing it's done right.

Tax Planning & Advice

Not just about filing taxes—I'll help you plan ahead. Quarterly estimates, retirement contributions, business deductions. Let's work together to keep more money in your pocket.

Payroll Services

Running a business with employees? I'll handle payroll processing, tax withholdings, and quarterly filings. Keep your team paid correctly and stay compliant with all employment tax requirements.

Financial Consulting

Whether you're buying a home, planning retirement, or starting a business, I'll help you understand the tax implications and optimize your financial decisions for long-term success.

Let's Make This Easy

Tax paperwork doesn't have to be stressful. Here's how we'll tackle it together:

Bring Your Stuff

Gather your tax documents, receipts, whatever you've got. Don't worry if it's messy—I'll help you sort it all out. You can drop by the office or email me.

I Do the Heavy Lifting

I'll review everything, find all the deductions you qualify for, and prepare your return. I'll call if I have questions, and I always explain what I'm doing.

Review & File

We'll go through your return together so you understand everything. Once you're happy with it, I'll file it electronically and keep you posted on your refund.
