About Serenity Nails & Spa

For over 8 years, Serenity Nails & Spa has been Ormond Beach's premier destination for luxury nail care and wellness. We believe in creating a tranquil escape where you can relax, rejuvenate, and emerge feeling beautiful inside and out.

Our skilled technicians use only the highest quality products and maintain the strictest sanitation standards. Every tool is sterilized, and we use disposable liners for all pedicure services to ensure your safety and comfort.
